About the Marrakesh

The Marrakesh is a meditarranian resturant founded in 1989.

Our goal is to provide both superior customer experience and tremendous value for our customers. The owner had over 30 years of experience in the restaurant business and is passionate about exceeding your expectations. The Marrakesh is:
